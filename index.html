About

I am a junior web developer, avid runner, and proud dog godfather. In the not too distant past, I was a senior associate at one of the Northwest’s largest registered investment advisors, but after working in the industry for 5 years and obtaining my CFA and CFP credentials, I realized that I had lost my passion for the work. Upon coming to this realization, I assessed my options and after much thought and research, I decided to pursue a career in web development and enroll in General Assembly’s full time immersive web development program. I first discovered my interest in coding during my senior year of college when I audited a computer science course. It immediately appealed to my analytical nature and love of problem solving, but at the time, I had already committed to a career in finance. Fast forward five years, and I’m thrilled to have the opportunity to revisit my old passion. The transition has been stressful, demanding and at times overwhelming, but it’s easily been the most fun I’ve ever had professionally, and it’s left me eager to learn more. Moving forward, I’m excited to continue to build my skills and join a team of energetic developers who share my passions for problem solving, learning, and getting things done!
